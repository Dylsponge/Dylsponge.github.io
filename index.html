THE BEAUTIFUL ISLAND OF TANITI

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.

The island offers many different ammenties for all visitors. The island offers 10 restaurants mainly offering local fish, but there are a few american and pan-asian dining options available as well. Taniti offers many different lodging arrangments from small hotels and bed and breakfasts and even a 4 star resort. Many people visit Taniti for the  beautiful beaches, rainforest and visit the islands volcano; however, there are many numerous other options for entertainment from pubs, dance clubs to art galleries you will be certain to find a way to enjoy your time on the island. Bus and Boat tours will show you all of the different wonderous sights of the island, or you can rent a taxi or bike and explore the island for yourself. Many visitors arrive through air travel but occasional cruiseships dock in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night to enjoy the island life.                                           

ENTERTAINMENT 

you can visit a local history museum, go on chartered fishing tours, snorkeling, zip-lining in the rainforest, visiting several pubs, including a microbrewery, dancing at a new dance club are just a few of the activites offered by the island    

TRANSPORTATION 

 Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island. Taxis are available in Taniti City, and rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport.

LODGING 

 Taniti has a wide variety of lodging that ranges from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort
