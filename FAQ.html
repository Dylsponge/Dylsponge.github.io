Frequently Asked Questions About the Island

  • There are power outlets and they are 120 volt, the same as in the United States
  • Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between Midnight and 9:00am
  • The legal drinking age is 18 but it is not strictly enforced
  • Many younger Tanitians speak fluent english, less english is spoken in more rural areas and by older generations
  • There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees
  • Violent crime is very rate in Taniti, but with tourism increasing there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes
  • Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants are closed on holidays
  • Taniti uses the U.S Dollar as its standard currency, but many buisnesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange and many businessess accept major credit cards
